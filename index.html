Games or Studying?

DO BOTH WITH STEMANIA

STEMANIA Website offers a variety of mathematical concepts that will help every student in the fundamentals of mathematics and needed to use in STEM specialized subjects.

Topics include: Common Unit Conversions, Fractions, Basic Algebra, Pythagorean Theorem, and Trigonometry. Most of these topics are tackled during Junior High School days. However, due to different circumstances, students are still having difficulties dealing with these concepts. Through the use of this website, these basic mathematical concepts will be used in real-life scenarios to show how these concepts can be applied in real-life situations. Problems presented on this website will help students not only in dealing with their math-related activities but also in dealing with the application of their learning in the real world.

Fraction

A fraction is a representation of a part of a whole using a numerator and a denominator, allowing for mathematical operations like addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.

Basic Algebra

Basic algebra focuses on mathematical operations and structures, using symbols and letters to represent numbers and quantities.

Trigonometry

Trigonometry is a branch of mathematics that deals with the relationships and calculations involving angles and the lengths of triangles.

Pythagorean Theorem

The Pythagorean theorem, named after Pythagoras, states that the squares of the two legs of a right triangle are equal to the square of the hypotenuse.

Unit Conversion

Unit conversion is the process of converting a value from one unit of measurement to another to establish an equivalent quantity.
